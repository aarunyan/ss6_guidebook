Pre-train → Fine-tune Paradigm
1
Pre-training
· Massive data (from internet, etc.)
· No labels needed
· Learns language + world knowledge
· High compute required
· Done once
2
Fine-tuning
· Much less data
· Task-specific labels
· Adapts model to task
· Low compute cost
· Multiple tasks possible
3
Inference
· Real-world usage
· Prompt → Response
· No more training
· Fast + efficient
· Deploy anywhere
